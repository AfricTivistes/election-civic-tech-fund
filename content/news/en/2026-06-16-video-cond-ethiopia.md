## Strengthening CSOs in Amhara

The **Consortium for Networking and Development (COND)** works toward transparent and peaceful electoral participation in Ethiopia's Amhara region, with support from the Election Civic Tech Fund.

The project strengthens youth and women-led civil society organizations (CSOs) through digital training, networking tools, and a database system for electoral observation.

## Approach

- Digital training for local CSOs
- Networking between civil society organizations
- Deployment of electoral observation tools
- Database for tracking electoral processes

## Funding

The Election Civic Tech Fund finances COND with **€15,000** to promote active CSO involvement in electoral governance in Amhara, Ethiopia.
