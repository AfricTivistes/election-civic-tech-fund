# Official Launch of the Election Civic Tech Fund

The **Election Civic Tech Fund** is proud to officially launch, a pan-African initiative aimed at strengthening electoral processes through citizen-driven technological innovation.

## A €175,000 Budget for Democratic Innovation

With a total budget of €175,000, the Fund will support 12 innovative projects across 14 African countries, with a particular focus on youth and technology.

## Key Figures

- **€175,000** total budget
- **14** African countries targeted
- **12** selected projects
- **9** major projects (€15,000)
- **3** micro-grants (€5,000)

## Led by Trusted Partners

The Fund is led by **AHEAD Africa**, designed and managed by **AfricTivistes**, and powered by the **Digital Democracy Initiative (DDI)** with support from the European Union, Norway, and Denmark.
