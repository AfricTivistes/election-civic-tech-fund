## Citizen Electoral Platform of Senegal

**Vie Publique Sénégal** is developing an open-source digital platform for electoral information and monitoring, made possible through the Election Civic Tech Fund.

The platform combines open data, electoral mapping, a public API, and OCR to enable citizens to access real-time information on elections, candidates, and results.

## Technology Innovation

- Open data and transparency of electoral data
- Interactive polling station mapping
- Open API for developers and researchers
- Automated processing of official documents via OCR

## Fund Support

The Election Civic Tech Fund finances this project with **€15,000** to facilitate transparency and real-time access to electoral information in Senegal.
