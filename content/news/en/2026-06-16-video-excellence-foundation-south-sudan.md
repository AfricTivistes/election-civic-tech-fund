## Excellence Foundation for South Sudan

As an Election Civic Tech Fund beneficiary, **Excellence Foundation for South Sudan** is deploying a unique electoral fact-checking initiative in South Sudan.

The project trains young journalists, students, and the general public in fact-checking and media literacy. Through a digital platform, training programs, and scholarships, the organization encourages the production of reliable content and fights electoral disinformation.

## Expected Impact

- A network of trained fact-checkers across the country
- School media clubs created to embed a fact-checking culture
- Digital platform accessible to citizens during electoral periods

The Election Civic Tech Fund supports this project with **€15,000** to strengthen citizen trust and transparency in South Sudan's electoral process.
