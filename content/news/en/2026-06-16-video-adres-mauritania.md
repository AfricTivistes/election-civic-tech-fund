## Legal Support for Youth on Electoral Processes

**ADRES – Association for Development and Social Resilience** supports young people in Nouakchott in accessing electoral legal information, with support from the Election Civic Tech Fund.

The project combines a WhatsApp chatbot, a digital citizen observatory, and a documentary to equip young Mauritanians to navigate the legal dimensions of elections.

## Tools Deployed

- **WhatsApp Chatbot**: instant answers to electoral legal questions
- **Citizen Observatory**: tracking and reporting of irregularities
- **Documentary**: large-scale awareness campaign

## Impact

ADRES aims to promote responsible citizenship and prevent electoral tensions in Mauritania, backed by **€15,000** from the Election Civic Tech Fund.
