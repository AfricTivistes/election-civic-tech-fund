## My Vote My Voice 2.0

**Actions for Development and Empowerment (ADE)** is one of 12 projects supported by the Election Civic Tech Fund in Cameroon. Their *My Vote My Voice 2.0* initiative combines civic technology and civic education for more transparent and inclusive elections.

The project deploys a mobile reporting app, citizen podcasts, and training programs to encourage active participation of youth and women in Cameroonian elections.

## What You'll See in the Video

The ADE team presents their tools, field training sessions, and early results of their work with Cameroonian communities.

## Funding

The Election Civic Tech Fund supports ADE with **€15,000** to transform citizen participation in Cameroon.
